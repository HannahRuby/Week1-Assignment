Welcome

Here at Javis' Farm Shop we sell the very best meat and local produce, ensuring our customers get the highest quality possible. Our family have been farming the same land for over 300 years and run a mixed stock farm, with welfare being our top priority.

The latest news,announcements from our team

When Javis Farm Shop was launched in 2006, it was out of a genuine passion for providing good quality fresh, locally sourced food and drink. We’re lucky that here, in Nottinghamshire’s historic Welbeck estate, we get to work with the most amazing community of food producers that call this estate their home. The estate’s food producers share our passion for great food and drink.
